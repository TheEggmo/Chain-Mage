You are the Chain Mage.

Descent down the dungeon using your dexterity and magic chains.
Vanquish enemies by joining them together and dragging them into eachother.
Reach the deepest chamber and defeat the Stone Golem.

Play using WASD and mouse.

Made for the GMTK 2021 gamejam.
Programming and game design by Bartłomiej "Eggmo" Płókarz.
Art by Piotr "Zane" Wiśniewski.
